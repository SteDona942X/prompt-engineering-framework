## 🔬 6-Phase Methodology

Our systematic approach transforms prompt development from intuition to process:

1. **Multidisciplinary Ideation** - Cross-domain concept generation
2. **Structured Extraction** - Convert ideas to actionable components
3. **Transversal Expansion** - Lateral thinking and perspective shifts
4. **Content Synthesis** - Merge insights and eliminate redundancies
5. **Structural Refinement** - Production-ready optimization
6. **Validation & Deployment** - Testing and maintenance protocols

## 🛠️ Usage Examples

### Business Analysis
```markdown
# Using Evaluative Template for Market Research
Ruolo: Agisci come senior market analyst
Task: Valuta questo business plan su tre criteri:
- Viabilità commerciale (peso: 40%)
- Fattibilità tecnica (peso: 35%)
- Potenziale di mercato (peso: 25%)

Output: Punteggio 1-10 per criterio + raccomandazioni
```

### Content Creation
```markdown
# Using Instructional Template for Content
Ruolo: Agisci come content strategist
Compito: Genera piano editoriale per LinkedIn
Input: Settore {{industry}}, Audience {{target}}
Output: 10 post ideas con hook e CTA
Vincoli: Max 2000 caratteri per post
```
